Tutoring Overview

For the fall of 2020, I am offering a math tutoring service to help students achieve high grades and understand material more clearly. For $20/hour, we can meet (virtually over Zoom or in-person at UNC-Chapel Hill) and talk about your class and figure out how to position you to succeed in your class! With over 4 years of tutoring experience, both privately and through the UNC Math Help Center, I offer a broad range of experience to help you. I am qualified to tutor in the following classes:

  • MATH 110, 129, 231, 232, 233, 381, and 547
  • STOR 155, 215, 305, 415, 435, 455, and 565
  • COMP 110, 116, 401, 410, 411, 426, 455, and 550

Sporting a 3.93 GPA in these classes, I have demonstrated a proficiency to understand concepts in each of these courses. Not only am I academically proficient, however, but I also have tangible stories of students improving their grades over the course of a semester.

A Tutoring Story

In my first official experience of tutoring, I was a senior in high school tutoring a college freshman in Pre-Calculus. His mother approached me and asked if I could tutor her son by reteaching concepts to him. Currently bordering a failing grade, his mother was concerned that he may have to retake the class, but asked if I could try to help him simply get a D. Over the course of the semester, I went over to his house once a week for about 3 hours. Through the semester, not only did we get to develop a friendship, but he also saw his grade improve to a B!
